The Fluffy bag is a handcrafted shopper that is light, fluffy, and spacious. My love for fabrics, which I seek out on my travels, has given rise to this new adventure. The silk Ikat patterns come from Turkey, while the jacquard fabrics are Italian and English. These particular materials allow me to create chromatic combinations that change with the seasons. The Fluffy bag is entirely made in Italy, each bag is numbered, and the patterned fabric on the front contrasts with the back; the inner lining, handles, and shoulder strap play with combinations of patterns and colors, enhancing the soft effect of the padding. These are unique pieces, indeed, each one is different from the other. They are chic, feminine, versatile, and essential. As a glamorous detail, each bag has velvet ribbons tied to the handle that create a chromatic contrast. The designs of the Fluffy bags seek a timeless harmony; everything is perfect in its imperfection, everything is precise in its imprecision.
